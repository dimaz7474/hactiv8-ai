Most Common Payment Methods

Overview
Based on the provided sales data, the most common payment method is QRIS. While Transfer and Tunai (Cash) are also used, QRIS accounts for the vast majority of transactions.

For example, on April 1, 2025, QRIS was used for 26 out of 30 transactions. This trend remains consistent across April, May, and June, indicating strong reliance on QRIS.

Data reflects transaction records from April to June 2025.
